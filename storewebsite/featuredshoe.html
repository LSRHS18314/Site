Jordan Retro 11 Bred

The Jordan Retro 11 Bred is the most popular colorway of the most popular shoe on the market. Pictured above, the Bred colorway features a dominantly black shoe, with the logo and sole in red. The Retro 11's have accounted for an astonishing 50% of our sales this past month, and therefore easily was chosen as our featured shoe of the month.

Jordan Retro 11 on Court

The Jordan Retro 11's were first exposed to the world in 1995, when Michael Jordan wore them in an NBA game. Due to the NBA's strict dress code, Michael Jordan had to wear the Bred 11 colorway in his games, or he recieved a fine. He had to wear the Bred 11 colorway because at the time it was an NBA guideline that your shoes have to match your uniform, and the Bred 11 colorway matches perfectly with the Chicago Bull's uniform.

Jordan Retro 11; Other Colorways

Although the Bred 11 is the most popular colorway, there are many other models of the Jordan 11 in high demand. One example of another colorway is the Concord 11's. The Concord 11's are largely white, with black accents on the logo and the lower part of the shoe. What makes the Concord so unique is the purple sole of the shoe. There are many other colorways in high demand as well.
